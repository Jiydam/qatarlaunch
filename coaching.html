Coaching

Overview of our Executive Coaching Services

Launch is committed to development in all aspects of your professional life. We are delighted to offer our executive coaching services to you. The information below explains a little more about this service.

What is Coaching?

Coaching is professional development, offered through the experience of a one to one relationship between you, the client, and our experienced executive coach. Coaching is deeply personal and bespoke to your needs. Coaching is an opportunity to develop in areas that are specific and personal to you and your professional goals. The quality and nature of the coaching relationship we offer provides you with an opportunity to experience deeper learning and a permanent change in your professional life.

Who is it For?

Our philosophy at Launch is that everyone who is seeking development can benefit from executive coaching. Due to the nature of the one to one coaching relationship, great results can be achieved in less time and last more permanently than undertaking generic development in group facilitated learning. We offer experience in coaching at all professional stages, career types, work settings, and work styles, including self-employed, family business and corporate settings.
